We may use third-party analytics services to help understand how the App is used and how we can improve it. These services may collect technical data (e.g., device type, OS version) but do not personally identify you to us.

We do not sell or share your personal data with advertisers or unrelated third parties.

To request data access or deletion, contact us at: support@emmamedia.hr. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe (typically within 30 days).
