Privacy Policy — Lumbra

Effective date: October 13, 2025

Controller: Emma Media d.o.o. (Croatia)

Contact: support@emmamedia.hr

Service: Lumbra mobile application and related websites/services (collectively, the “Service”).

Plain-language summary

We collect only what we need to run the app and personalize your back-care plan (account info, onboarding answers, progress, and basic device/diagnostic data). Health-related data is processed only with your explicit consent and is never sold. Payments are handled by Apple/Google. You can access, export, correct, or delete your data anytime in the app (Settings → Privacy) or via our website.

1) What data we collect

A) Data you provide

  • Account & identifiers: email, password (stored hashed), country/region, language, subscription status, support communications.
  • Onboarding & profile inputs: age range, height/weight range, fitness level, pain areas, goals, contraindications, and other optional details you choose to share.
  • Workout & progress: completed sessions, XP, streaks, badges, Freeze tokens, schedules, preferences, reminders.
  • Feedback: ratings, feature requests, issue reports, optional comments.

B) Health-related data (special category)

Some onboarding answers and progress markers may reveal health information (e.g., back pain intensity, mobility limitations). We process health-related (special category) data only with your explicit consent and only to deliver core functionality (plan personalization and safety). We do not use health data for advertising, marketing, or sale.

C) Data collected automatically (device/technical)

  • Device & app info: device model, OS version, app version, language, time zone, crash logs, basic diagnostics.
  • Usage analytics: feature interactions, screens viewed, session duration/retention, aggregated performance metrics.
  • Approximate location: derived from IP for fraud/security/locale; we do not collect precise GPS unless you explicitly enable a feature that needs it.
  • Advertising identifiers (IDFA/AAID): only if you opt in to advertising/personalization where required. (We never combine health data with ad identifiers.)

D) Data from third parties (only if you opt in)

  • Apple Health / HealthKit and/or Google Health Connect/Fit: activity/health data you authorize us to read/write. We do not use this data for ads/marketing/data brokerage.
  • Sign-in providers (e.g., Sign in with Apple/Google): name (optional) and email as allowed by your settings.
  • App stores/payment processors: subscription status and transaction confirmations. We never receive your full card details.

2) How we use data (purposes)

  • Provide, operate, and personalize the Service (plan generation, progress tracking, preferences).
  • Safety & quality (contraindication checks, appropriate intensity adjustments, stability and misuse prevention).
  • Support & communications (tickets, service notices, password resets, policy updates).
  • Analytics & improvement (performance monitoring, crash diagnostics, A/B tests where applicable).
  • Payments & billing (subscription validation, receipts, refunds, tax/VAT as required).

3) Sharing and disclosures

We never sell your personal data.

We share data only as needed to operate the Service, for example with:

  • Service providers/processors (hosting, analytics/diagnostics, messaging, customer support, security, backups) under contract and confidentiality obligations.
  • Independent controllers such as Apple App Store / Google Play for payments and subscriptions, and Health platforms per your device permissions.
  • Legal/compliance (regulators, courts, tax authorities) when legally required.

4) Data deletion & your rights

You may request access, correction, export, restriction, or deletion of your data (depending on your location).

Delete your account: in the app via Settings → Privacy → Delete Account, or via our web form: https://emmamedia.hr/delete-account/.

You can also contact us at support@emmamedia.hr. After a confirmed deletion request, personal data is deleted typically within 30 days, except where we must retain certain records for legal obligations (e.g., tax/transaction records).

5) Retention

  • Account/profile/workout data: retained while your account is active; deleted within 30 days after confirmed deletion.
  • Diagnostics/crash logs: typically retained 90–180 days, then aggregated or deleted.
  • Transaction records: retained 5–10 years where required by law.
  • Backups: encrypted, rolling cycles; data expires according to retention schedules.

6) Security

We use appropriate administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards (encryption in transit, access controls, logging, and security practices). No system is perfectly secure, but we work to protect your information.

7) Children

The Service is not intended for children under 16 (or the age defined by local law). We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8) International transfers

We may process data internationally. Where required, we use lawful safeguards (e.g., contractual clauses) and supplementary measures.

9) Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The effective date above will be updated if material changes occur.
